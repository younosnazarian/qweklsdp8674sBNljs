About Younos Nazarian

Hi, I’m Younos Nazarian. I’m a Network Expert, Trainer, photographer, and entrepreneur.

This website is the home of my professional life. I write about networking technologies/concepts and how to implement them in the real world. I’m trying to demystify networking concepts and show you how to implement them on a network appliance.

To achieve that goal, I write articles and create videos to show you exactly how things work in action according to what I learned throughout all of my active years. My specific focus though is on Juniper Networks Junos operating system and SRX security gateways.

Regarding Juniper Networks, JNCIE-SEC (#314) is my latest achievement. I have ten years plus experience working with Juniper Networks products and 3000+ hours of instructing and training experience. I am privileged to work with the most prominent carriers/enterprises and train hundreds of people.

Please feel free to contact me for any training and consulting services.
I don’t claim to have all the answers, and I still have a lot to learn, but I’m happy to share what I’ve learned along the way.
